Someone once asked me: "Why do you even do this? What's your social impact, really?"
I told them: I'm not doing this for self-gratification. There is no monetary goal. I simply want to help people. That's the whole sentence.

What is life without experience?

I have this urge — a very consistent, very persistent urge — to put things in the right order. To build systems. To make things work. But equally, to break them when they stop serving people, and grow beyond them entirely. I think growing is one of the few things in life that is entirely self-determined. It has nothing to do with age, or experience, or permission. If you want to grow, just grow.

My heroes are my parents. No footnote needed.
